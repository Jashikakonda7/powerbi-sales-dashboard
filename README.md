# 📊 Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Problem Statement

Businesses generate large amounts of sales data, but without proper visualization, it becomes difficult to identify trends and make informed decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To build an interactive dashboard that helps analyze sales performance and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning
* Data Visualization
* DAX (Basic Measures)

---

## 📊 Features

✔ KPI Cards (Total Sales, Profit, Orders)
✔ Sales by Region and Category
✔ Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
✔ Top Products Performance
✔ Interactive Filters (Year, Category)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* West region consistently contributed the highest share of total revenue
* Technology category generated maximum sales across all regions
* Sales trends indicate peak performance during specific months
* Discounts have a noticeable impact on overall profitability

---

## 💼 Business Impact

* Helps identify high-performing regions and products
* Supports better decision-making using visual insights
* Enables quick analysis of trends and patterns
